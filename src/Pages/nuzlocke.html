1. Permadeath.

1.A. If a demon dies, they must be released from the party after the current battle ends.

1.B. You return to your last save if killed in battle. (Up for Discussion)

1.C.

2. One Demon per Area.

2.A. Upon entering a new area, ...

2.B. You can only obtain a demon once.

2.C.

3. No Demonic Compendium.

3.A. You cannot summon a demon from the compendium.

3.B. Fusion is allowed, but the resulting demon cannot be a demon alrady obtained, nor can the fusion ingrediencts be obtained again.

4. Exceptions to the Rules.

4.A. A demon who begs to join your party is allowed to join, if you have not already recruited one of said demon.

4.B. Demons who are forced into your party (Pixie), or the player is given a choice to recruit them (Black Frost, Dante) are allowed to do so.

4.C. The demons for sale by the merchants in the Labyrinth of Amala are allowed to be bought, since the demons in the Kalpas cannot be recruited.

5. Level Cap.

5.1. Your level can only be as high as the boss of the current area.
